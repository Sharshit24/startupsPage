Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hitech Billsoft billing software?
Hitech Billsoft is an easy-to-use billing software designed for startups, small businesses, retailers, wholesalers, restaurants, and service providers to manage invoicing, inventory, and sales efficiently.
Is there a free version of Hitech Billsoft available?
Yes, Hitech Billsoft offers a 100% free, fully functional limited version with no hidden charges, though it has some limitations on monthly invoices and certain features.
Can Hitech Billsoft be used offline?
Absolutely. Hitech Billsoft is an offline desktop application that works seamlessly without an internet connection, ensuring your billing operations are never interrupted.
How does Hitech Billsoft help with GST compliance?
Hitech Billsoft is 100% GST compliant, enabling you to generate multi-rate, multi-item GST invoices and automatically calculate GST and cess amounts. It also helps generate GSTR reports.
Is Hitech Billsoft an accounting software?
Hitech Billsoft is primarily a billing software focused on efficient invoicing, sales, and inventory management. While it assists with bookkeeping and tax filing, it's simpler and more beginner-friendly than comprehensive accounting software.
